About the Program:

Mahatma Jyothiba Phule Overseas Vidya Nidhi 2023, Telangana is an initiative by the Backward Classes Welfare Department, Government of Telangana for BC and EBC category students who want to pursue UG, PG, and Ph.D. level courses in abroad. The key objective of the scheme is to enable the benefit of higher education in foreign universities to meritorious students thereby providing them with an opportunity for better career prospects within the country. The selected candidates will receive a scholarship of up to INR 2 lakh or as mentioned in the admission letter (whichever is less). Additionally, students will receive one-way flight tickets (economic class) and visa charges.
